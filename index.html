About Us

A Cross brake parachute is to be used for assisting the wheel braking of Advance Medium Combat Aircraft (AMCA),This Simulation contains the detailed desing calulator of the Cross brake parachute, the spring loaded auxiliary parachute and the deployment bag.

Brake Parachute System Configuration

The brake parachute system consists three major sub systems:-

Main Parachute: to assist the normal wheel braking of the aircraft.
Auxiliary Parachute: to initiate the functioning of main parachute.
Pack Assembly: to house and properly deploy the main perachute.

Brake parachute system consists of pilot, auxiliary and main parachute which will be packed in canopy deployment bag. this system would be placed in the brake parachute cylindrical shape container, fitted at the rear end of aircraft.
